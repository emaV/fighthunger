INSTALL INSTRUCTIONS FOR SMTP.MODULE
----

1. Copy smtp.module to your Drupal modules/ folder.
2. Login at site administrator.
3. Activate smtp.module on the administer->modules page.
4. Fill in required settings on the administer->settings->smtp page.
5. Enjoy.

Notes:
This version of the module is designed for DRUPAL 4.7 only.
